---
title: "Setup a Server"
description: "A step-by-step guide about setting up a Server on RestoreCord."
icon: "server"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Server">
    > Head over to the Dashboard, Servers then click `Create Server`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Custom Bot">
    > Select your custom bot.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/setup_create-server_select-bot.png" />
    </Frame>
    >
    <Note>
        Make sure to close the popup windows after inviting the bot!
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your Discord Server">
    > After inviting your bot to your server, select the server you want to use RestoreCord.
    <Frame>
        <img src="/images/setup_create-server_select-server.png" />
    </Frame>
    >
    <Note>
        Feel free to change the server name to anything you want, that name will be shown on the verify link.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fix Permissions">
    > After inviting the bot, make sure that the integrated bot role is above the verify:
    <Frame>
        <img src="/images/setup_bot-permissions.png" />
    </Frame>
    >
    <Warning>
        Make sure that the bot has the Administrator permission, giving your custom bot an Administrator Role does **not** work.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the verify role">
    > Select the role you want your members to receive after verifying with RestoreCord.
    <Frame>
        <img src="/images/setup_create-server_select-role.png" />
    </Frame>
    >
    <Note>
        If your roles dont show up then make sure that the integrated bot role is above the verified role, click the "**click here**" button on the bottom to refresh the roles list.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check> You have succesfully added your Discord Server to RestoreCord! </Check>
