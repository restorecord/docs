## Setup Webhook Logs
<Warning>This may not work on mobile!</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Creating a Webhook">
    >First **edit** the channel you wish to use Webhook logs on. Go to **Integrations** then click **Create Webhook**.

    <Tip>We highly recommend setting this channel to **private**.</Tip>
    <Frame>
            <img src="/images/setup_webhook-logs_create.png"/>
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy Webhook URL">
    > After creating a webhook, **copy** the **Webhook URL** and go to the [RestoreCord Dashboard](https://restorecord.com/dashboard/server)
    <Frame>
            <img src="/images/setup_webhook-logs_copy-url.png"/>
    </Frame>
    >
    <Info>You may change the webhook name or profile picture to your preference.</Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Webhook to RestoreCord">
    > Go to the [RestoreCord Dashboard](https://restorecord.com/dashboard/server), edit your server on the Dashboard, scroll down and enable **Webhook logs**.

<Frame>
        <img src="/images/setup_webhook-logs_configure.png"/>
</Frame>
>
> After **enabling** webhook logs, **paste the Webhook URL** you have copied before and click **Save**.

  </Step>
</Steps>


<Check>You have successfully configured webhook logs for your RestoreCord Server!</Check>
